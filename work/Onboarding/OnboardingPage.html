Project: Onboarding POC

The objective of this new Onboarding initiative was to drive early member engagement when they first sign up through education of earn partners & redemptions opportunities. This Proof of Concept (POC) was developed to both showcase the user experience and interface design to senior stakeholders within the business as well as explore the technical feasibility of the concept in areas such as API integration and dynamic content fueled through Contentful CMS

Team Qantas Loyalty
Role Developer
Tools React, Typescript, Next.js

Proof of Concept

Multi-Page Onboarding engine

Introduce & Incentivise

The main goal of Onboarding was to ensure that post sign-up, new members wern't just thrown into a Loyalty ecosystem with no understanding of how to engage with the program. Thus through this cultivated experience the aim was to welcome them into the project and guide them through the key aspects through a points incentive

Earning & Redeeming points

The main focus of the onboarding experience was to educate members on how to earn and redeem points through key examples and showcases, these pages are exlored further in-depth in the next sections

Transition into the dashboard

The selections which members make during the onboarding experience are further presented in their My Account dashboard once they finalise onboarding, the aim of the final animation was to both reinforce the choices members have made alongside giving time for the back-end to process to data as to provide a seamless transition into the dashboard

Program Misunderstanding

Many Qantas Frequent Flyer members overestimate the points needed to redeem rewards, often by 2-5x. This showcase aims to educate members on actual redemption costs and highlights the variety of available rewards.

Points Slider

The above slider was designed to allow members to set a goal to earn towards. The interesting part when developing this was that the point milestones increased expontentially, and thus some additional calculations had tobe made to ensure the slider felt smooth and natural

API Integration

Leveraging the existing Qantas Rewards API that was developed by the Redemption team, the rewards showcased are dynamic and reflect real-time availability and point costs. This ensures members always see up-to-date information, and special offers can be highlighted during promotional periods

Earning comprehension

The aim of this page was to educate members on the various ways they can earn points through Qantas and its partners. The challenge with this was to ensure that new members wern't overwhelmed and they could easily identify ways that they could earn points based on their lifestyle

Back-end considerations

Members could select the specific partners they were interested in, these selections were then passed to the back-end in terms of idenftifiers which were then stored and later presented in their dashboard

Contentful Integration

As these partners are dynamic and subject to change, each partner was developed to be managed through Contentful CMS. This allowed non-technical team members to update partner information without needing developer intervention, ensuring the onboarding content remained current and relevant
