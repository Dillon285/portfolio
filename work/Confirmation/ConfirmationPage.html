Engage Early

The business understood the importance of engaging new members early in their journey. Thus the confirmation page was a key touchpoint to educate and excite members about the program, encouraging them to log in and explore

Functionality Improvements

Introduced new functionality to the confirmation page, such as a copy to clipboard button for the members who wanted to saved their membership number for later use - helpful for those on a mobile

Encourgaing login

Through the use of clear CTAs and value propositions, members were encouraged to log in and explore their new account. The log-in button was made prominent and the benefits of unlocking extra points was displayed
